ABOUT

The Project

If you have a maths equation that is too complicated or you are too tired, go to Ask and enter all the data that other users will need to do your exercises. You will find the answers to your questions on My Stuff

The Website

This site was created entirely by two high school students using HTML5, CSS3, Python, Django Web Framework and Bootstrap

How it works

Ask for your exercises in exchange for coins, which are earned by solving other people's problems. On the Ask page you enter all the data and pictures relating to your exercises, which will then be displayed on Earn by other users. The completed exercises will be displayed in My Stuff where you can say whether they were useful or not. If the answer was not useful you will get a refund.
